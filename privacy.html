Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to ExcuseGen ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at https://abdodady0201.github.io/excusegen/ (the "Site").

By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the Site.

Information We Collect

ExcuseGen is designed to be a lightweight, privacy-respecting tool. We collect the absolute minimum of information required to operate the Site.

  • Text input: Any text you enter into the "Your Situation" field is processed entirely within your browser. It is never transmitted to our servers and is never stored.
  • Local storage: We store a single numeric value in your browser's localStorage to track the number of excuses you have generated across sessions. This data never leaves your device.
  • Automatically collected data: Like most websites, our hosting platform (GitHub Pages) may automatically log standard server data including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and page visit times. This data is collected by GitHub and governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Cookies

ExcuseGen itself does not set any cookies directly. However, third-party services embedded on the Site (including analytics providers and advertising networks) may set cookies in your browser. These cookies are governed by the respective third parties' privacy policies.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain third-party features.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

We display advertisements provided by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Site and other websites on the internet. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use anonymised, aggregated analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with the Site (e.g., page views, bounce rate, session duration). No personally identifiable information is tied to this data.

Third-Party Links and Social Sharing

The Site includes social share buttons linking to WhatsApp, Twitter/X, and Facebook. These links direct you to external platforms governed by their own privacy policies. Clicking these links may allow those platforms to collect data about you. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party platforms you visit.

Children's Privacy

ExcuseGen is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

Data Security

Since ExcuseGen does not collect or store personal information on our servers, the risk of a data breach on our end is minimal. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since we do not store personal data on our servers, most of these rights are satisfied automatically. For any concerns, please contact us using the form on our Contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
